Stranger Things

About the Series

Year of Production: 2016

Platform: Netflix

Country: USA

Genre: horror, science fiction, fantasy, thriller, drama, detective

Director: Matt Duffer, Ross Duffer, Shawn Levy

Starring

Finn Wolfhard: Mike Wheeler

Gaten Matarazzo: Dustin Henderson

Noah Schnapp: Will Byers

Caleb McLaughlin: Lucas Sinclair

Millie Brown: Eleven

Summary

begins with the mysterious disappearance of Will Byers, a 12-year-old boy,
in the town of Hawkins, Indiana. At the same time,
a girl with paranormal abilities known as Eleven appears in town,
escaping from a laboratory where she was being experimented on.
